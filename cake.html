Chocolate Cake

Ingredients for the Cake:

  • 200g plain flour
  • 200g granulated sugar
  • 75g cocoa powder
  • 1½ tsp baking powder
  • 1½ tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 240ml milk
  • 120ml vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 240ml boiling water

Instructions:

For the Frosting:

  • 100g unsalted butter, softened
  • 200g powdered sugar
  • 50g cocoa powder
  • 60ml milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

1. Bake the Cake:

  • Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease and flour two 20cm round cake pans.
  • In a large bowl, sift together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  • Add the eggs, milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Mix until well combined.
  • Slowly add the boiling water to the batter, mixing continuously. The batter will be quite thin, but this is normal.
  • Divide the batter evenly between the two prepared cake pans.
  • Bake in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cakes comes out clean.
  • Let the cakes cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then remove them from the pans and transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.

2. Make the Frosting:

  • In a medium bowl, beat the softened butter until creamy.
  • Gradually add the powdered sugar and cocoa powder, alternating with the milk, until the frosting is smooth and creamy.
  • Mix in the vanilla extract and salt. If the frosting is too thick, add a little more milk; if its too thin, add more powdered sugar.

3. Assemble the Cake:

  • Place one of the cooled cake layers on a serving plate.
  • Spread a layer of frosting on top of the cake.
  • Place the second cake layer on top and spread frosting over the top and sides of the cake.
  • If desired, decorate with chocolate shavings or sprinkles.
